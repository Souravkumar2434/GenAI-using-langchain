Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players. Originating in England, it has become a global sport, especially in countries like India, Australia, and England. The game involves batting, bowling, and fielding, with teams taking turns to score runs. Formats include Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20). Each format varies in duration and strategy, offering diverse experiences for players and fans. Cricket emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and endurance, and is governed internationally by the ICC. It enjoys a massive fanbase and deep cultural significance in many nations.
